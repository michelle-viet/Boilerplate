Swingporium
Electro Swing
Music Festival

augmented reality (ar) / branding / illustration

Swingporium celebrates electro swing, a musical genre that combines the swing and jazz eras of the 1920s to '40s with the electric beat bass and beats of house and hip-hop styles. With the liveliness of swing and sound tuned to the modern ear, the festival invites today’s dancers to hit the floor. The event also lets festival goers immerse themselves into vintage social life added features including cabaret, burlesque, circus and magic shows.
